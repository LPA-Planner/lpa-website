About LPA Planner

Independent planning enforcement specialist

LPA Planner is an independent town planning service providing clear, concise written advice on enforcement, appeals, and compliance matters. With over 13 years’ experience working inside Local Planning Authorities, LPA Planner bridges professional insight and accessible, plain-English guidance for homeowners, architects and developers.

LPA Planner is a trademarked trading name, operating as a sole trader business.

Current Professional Position

LPA Planner currently provides independent advice while also working within Local Planning Authorities. Some case types may not be accepted where conflicts of interest or contractual obligations arise. All enquiries are screened individually to ensure compliance with professional and contractual requirements before any service is accepted.
