About LPA Planner

Independent planning enforcement and development management specialist

LPA Planner is an independent town planning service providing clear, concise planning advice on enforcement, appeals, and compliance matters. With over 13 years’ experience working inside Local Planning Authorities, LPA Planner bridges professional insight and accessible, plain-English guidance for homeowners, architects and developers.

“LPA Planner” is a trademarked trading name, operating as a sole trader business.

Professional Standards

Drawing on over a decade of professional experience in planning enforcement and development management, LPA Planner combines a clear understanding of both the investigative and decision-making sides of the planning system. That includes assessing complex applications, presenting cases at committee, and guiding enforcement action through to appeal or legal resolution.

The approach is detailed but balanced, and is shaped by years of advising senior officers, legal teams, and councillors on proportionate and defensible outcomes. Every service is delivered with the same care, clarity, and professional rigour expected within local government, ensuring advice that is reliable, impartial, and firmly grounded in planning law and practice.

LPA Planner operates with strict professional integrity and independence. Every enquiry is screened to ensure there are no conflicts of interest and that all advice remains impartial, compliant, and aligned with professional obligations.
