Planning Can Be Complex

Town planning exists to balance the needs of development, neighbours, and the wider community. But the process can feel intimidating — with strict deadlines, technical terminology, and sometimes conflicting advice.

LPA Planner aims to make the process as clear and straightforward as possible. Whether you’ve received a Notice, had an Application refused, or want to check if you need Permission, you’ll get practical, actionable advice you can trust.

About LPA Planner

LPA Planner is an independent Planning Enforcement specialist with over 13 years’ experience working in local government.

  • Drafted, served, and defended formal Planning Notices — including Enforcement Notices (ENs), Breach of Condition Notices (BCNs), and Planning Contravention Notices (PCNs).
  • Led successful prosecutions for Planning offences and given evidence in court.
  • Assessed a wide range of Planning Applications — from householder extensions and Lawful Development Certificates (LDCs) to complex commercial schemes and major developments.
  • Represented councils at Planning Committees and Public Inquiries.
  • Advised Planning Officers, Councillors, and Legal teams on Enforcement strategy and decision-making.

I now bring that experience to help homeowners, businesses, and developers navigate the Planning system with clarity and confidence.
