Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Introduction

LPA Planner is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This policy explains what data I collect, how I use it, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. Data I Collect

  • Contact details – if you email me, I collect your email address and any information you include in your message.
  • Client information – if we work together, I may collect planning documents, site information, or other data you choose to share for the purpose of providing advice.

3. How I Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide planning advice.
  • To maintain basic records for administration and legal compliance.
  • To improve the services I offer.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

I process your personal data on the basis of:

  • Legitimate interests – to respond to enquiries and deliver services.
  • Legal obligations – where required for record-keeping or compliance.

5. Sharing Your Data

I do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. I may share data only if required by law (for example, in response to a court order).

6. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely and only kept for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose it was collected for. I take reasonable steps to protect your data from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data I hold about you.
  • Ask for incorrect data to be corrected.
  • Request that your data is deleted where legally possible.
  • Object to certain types of processing.
To exercise your rights, contact me using the details below.

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, you can contact me at:
